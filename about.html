About

Created by someone who loves strange visuals, glitchy vibes, old-school games, and weird playlists. Everything here is an experiment — sometimes pretty, sometimes broken. That’s the point.

This site is hand-coded, mostly in the dark, probably with too many tabs open and music blasting in the background.

If you’re lost, confused, or curious — good. Welcome to the void.
